Sales Development Representative

BreezoMeter is looking for a motivated Sales Development Representative that has
demonstrable experience in generating leads and opportunities by reaching and
establishing relationships with leadership level contacts.

The Sales Development Representative is the first point of contact for our prospects and is responsible for generating lead volume that will bring new business to the company. In this position, you will have the unique occasion to build and deploy your inbound and outbound strategy.

Candidates should have experience in sales development in companies with a B2B and SAAS/DAAS business model and should have strong english communication skills.

Responsibilities & Activities

  • Generate new business opportunities to fuel the SMB and Enterprise pipelines by scheduling meetings with qualified prospects.
  • Work closely with Account Executives to move prospects through pipeline and closing.
  • Manage a variety of sales automation and online prospecting tools
  • Work with both inbound leads as well as generating outbound leads to consistently over achieve a monthly lead quota.
  • Achieve or exceed quarterly quotas of qualified opportunities generated.
  • Conduct a large volume of outbound calls and emails (50+ daily) to customers and prospects to gather information and initiate the first step in identifying those interested parties who are interested in learning more about the company’s products and services.
  • Daily integration with HubSpot CRM to adopt accounts, leads, contacts, and opportunities.
  • Conduct research to expand prospect list.

Requirements

  • 2 years minimum experience as an SDR or Inside Sales for companies with SaaS/DaaS business model.
  • Experience in sales in companies with a B2B and SAAS/DAAS business model .
  • Very high English level is a must.
  • Good verbal and written (email) communications skills.
  • Dynamic telephone presence.
  • Drive, conscientiousness and perseverance to conduct outbound contacts daily.
  • Tech Savvy.
  • Bachelor’s Degree required in Marketing, Business or other relevant fields.
  • Passion to impact human health & wellness.

Travel Requirements

BreezoMeter's office is based in Haifa and the candidate needs to have flexibility for occasional business travel abroad.
