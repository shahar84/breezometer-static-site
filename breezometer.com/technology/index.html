State-of-the-art Technology

Our Advanced technology allows us to keep pace with the complex flow dynamics that change the air, several times a day from street to street.

1

Collecting:

Thousands of sources worldwide are gathered into our powerful big data infrastructure.

2

Validating:

Every hour, over 1.6TB of data are validated and organized to produce 420 million geographical data points.

3

Modeling:

7.1 billion compound calculations are then performed by proprietary dispersion algorithms, using machine learning techniques.

4

The Result:

Ultra-accurate, hyperlocal air quality data is at your fingertip, available through a simple API
